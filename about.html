HEY,
NICE TO MEET YOU

Who am I?

My name is Chloé Michel, I am a 21-years-old graphic designer and student in digital graphic design at Lycée Jacques Prévert, Boulogne. I was born and raised in the suburb of Paris with a pencil in my hand.

How did I get here?

I started playing with Photoshop, HTML and CSS early but it’s only during high school that I realised that my passion for graphic design could become my career. So I got into the prestigious École Estienne, Paris to grow my roots in design and I am now fully exploring my capabilities in graphic design at Lycée Prévert

What am I passionate about?

I’ve always had a thing for good looking websites and coding. But I also love videos that take you elsewhere, to the rythm of a music or of its visuals. I aim at being polyvalent in my work as I think good-designer-me is someone that can take any subject of any kind from anywhere and make it her own.

Something crazy I've done?

At 14, I decided I had to change my everyday life and at 15 I flew off alone to Avon Lake, Ohio to live a school year as an exchange student.
